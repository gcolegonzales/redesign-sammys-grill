Our story

A Baton Rouge tradition since '88.

Sammy's Grill has been the go-to spot for tailgating, watching LSU, and a proper Louisiana meal since 1988. Everything's built from scratch — the roux, the remoulade, the po'boys dressed on real Leidenheimer bread.

What started on Highland Road grew into a second home in Prairieville. Same recipes, same welcome: come hungry, leave family.

“Fresh Louisiana seafood, Cajun favorites, and burgers & po'boys built from scratch.”
